Rue de Savoie 59, Saint-Gilles, Brussels, Belgium, 1060

Casual restaurant making organic and vegan all-day breakfast & brunch foods like toasts, bircher, banana bread, acai bowls, and sandwiches. Serves a variety of cakes plus coffee and different drinks. Est. 2019. Open Mon-Fri 07:00-17:00, Sat-Sun 11:00-15:00.

High price, uninventive food and freezing cold - Edit

We were very excited to find out there was another vegan coffee place in Brussels and as usual we checked it out. The pros: cozy and relax environment, banana bread and the croissant were really good. The cons: EUR 4.50 for a micro-cup of oat milk with spirulina served lukewarm (similar prices for hot chocolate), despite being full with people the room was freezing cold and even with our coats we were not comfortable, we didn't eat it but we saw the avocado toast for EUR 9 on other tables completely overpriced. Please readjust the heating, recalibrate some of the prices of your offerings or increase your hot (hot not lukewarm!) beverage portion and we will be happy to go back.
